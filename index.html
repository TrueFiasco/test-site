About Me

I've been crafting abstract and geometric digital art since I was 10, starting with fractals in Photoshop and graduating to coding fractals in Apophysis. Once I got my hands on a gaming laptop capable of rendering enough frames, I dove headfirst into animation.

My style spans minimal, glitch, geometric, and sci-fi aesthetics, though I've created countless bespoke VJ sets tailored to a wide range of events. For installations, I love creating experiences that are playful, highly responsive, and intuitively interactive.

With 8+ years of TouchDesigner experience and two decades in creative coding, I've worked at major music festivals like Shambala, Boom Town, and Balter. I've held residencies at clubs including Tunnel Club (Birmingham) and KOKO (Camden), handling visuals and lighting. I've also helped university students achieve firsts in their final projects.

I've been involved in every aspect of event production—from concept and booking to promoting, stage building, rigging lights and audio, decorating, and creating immersive reactive visuals. TouchDesigner is my tool of choice because it excels at creating interactive real-time visuals. If TouchDesigner is part of a project, it's almost certainly a pretty cool project!
