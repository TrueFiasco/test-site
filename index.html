About

I've spent two decades in creative coding and 8+ years mastering TouchDesigner, bringing interactive installations to life at major festivals like Shambala and Boom Town, with residencies at clubs like KOKO Camden. From minimal glitch aesthetics to full-scale immersive experiences, I create playful, highly responsive installations that feel intuitive and alive.

Whether you need expert consultation on your interactive project, want to learn TouchDesigner from someone who's done it all (first lesson free!), or need full project creation for an installation—I've got you covered. TouchDesigner is the best tool for real-time interactive visuals, and if you're using it, you're probably working on something pretty cool. Let's make it happen.
